# Digital Lending Risk Analytics & Credit Policy Recommendation

## Overview

This project was developed as a competition-style case study focused on digital lending risk assessment and credit policy optimization.

Using a synthetic lending portfolio of 5,000 customers, the analysis evaluates borrower segments, credit scores, loan characteristics, delinquency behavior, and default patterns to generate actionable lending policy recommendations.

The project demonstrates practical skills in:

* Credit Risk Analytics
* Lending Portfolio Analysis
* Data-Driven Decision Making
* Business Strategy Development
* Financial Data Interpretation

---

## Business Problem

Digital lenders must balance growth with risk management.

The objective of this project is to identify key factors associated with borrower delinquency and default, evaluate portfolio performance across customer segments, and propose policy recommendations that improve loan quality while maintaining business growth.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains 5,000 customer lending records with variables including:

| Feature             | Description                 |
| ------------------- | --------------------------- |
| customer_id         | Unique customer identifier  |
| segment             | Prime, Near Prime, Subprime |
| geography           | Customer region             |
| income              | Annual income               |
| credit_score        | Creditworthiness indicator  |
| loan_type           | Product category            |
| loan_amount         | Loan principal              |
| tenure              | Loan duration               |
| acquisition_channel | Customer acquisition source |
| days_past_due       | Delinquency measure         |
| default_flag        | Loan default indicator      |

## Key Business Questions

* Which borrower segments exhibit the highest risk?
* How does credit score impact loan performance?
* What lending characteristics are associated with default?
* Which acquisition channels produce higher-quality customers?
* How can credit policy be improved to reduce portfolio risk?

---

## Analytical Focus Areas

### Customer Segmentation

Comparison of Prime, Near Prime, and Subprime borrowers.

### Credit Risk Assessment

Evaluation of delinquency and default indicators.

### Loan Portfolio Analysis

Assessment of loan size, tenure, and repayment performance.

### Acquisition Strategy

Performance comparison across customer acquisition channels.

---

## Recommendations

Based on portfolio analysis, lenders should:

* Strengthen underwriting standards for higher-risk segments.
* Implement risk-based pricing strategies.
* Increase monitoring of delinquent accounts.
* Improve acquisition-channel quality controls.
* Develop predictive risk scoring models for proactive intervention.

---

## Business Impact

The insights generated through this analysis can help lenders:

* Reduce default rates
* Improve portfolio quality
* Optimize credit policies
* Increase profitability
* Enhance risk management frameworks
